# Privacy Policy

Status: working product draft, not final legal advice.
Last updated: 2026-06-18.

This Privacy Policy explains how Market Signal AI collects, uses, stores, shares, and deletes personal data in connection with the website, account dashboard, Telegram flows, ticker analysis, reports, API access, payments, and support.

## 1. Data Controller

The legal entity, registered address, privacy contact, and data protection contact must be added before launch.

## 2. Data We Collect

Market Signal AI may collect:

- account data: name, email, language, country, login identifiers, Telegram user ID, Telegram username, Telegram display name;
- subscription data: plan, status, trial status, renewal period, cancellation status, provider customer ID, provider subscription ID;
- payment data: payment status, invoice or receipt references, transaction metadata, chargeback or dispute status; full card details should be handled only by the payment provider;
- Telegram data: bot interactions, WebApp init data, selected channels, country/language choices, chat identifiers, commands, button clicks, join requests, and messages sent to bots where applicable;
- user settings: preferred tickers, risk profile, delivery preferences, selected countries, selected languages;
- analysis data: ticker requests, prompts, report outputs, analysis history, request IDs, timestamps, error states;
- API data: API key prefix, API key hash, creation time, revocation time, usage events, endpoint, rate-limit counters, IP-derived security metadata where needed;
- audit logs: account changes, subscription events, API key actions, admin actions, security events, delivery events;
- device and technical data: IP address, user agent, approximate location, cookies, session identifiers, diagnostics, crash logs;
- support data: messages, attachments, contact details, issue history.

## 3. How We Use Data

We use personal data to:

- create and manage accounts;
- provide subscription access;
- deliver Telegram links, bot messages, and channel access where allowed;
- process ticker analysis and reports;
- provide API access and enforce rate limits;
- maintain billing, invoices, receipts, refunds, and dispute handling;
- secure the service and prevent abuse;
- monitor system health and audit important events;
- respond to support requests;
- improve product quality and reliability;
- comply with legal obligations.

## 4. Legal Bases

Where GDPR-style rules apply, the expected legal bases are:

- contract performance for account, subscription, API, Telegram delivery, and reports;
- legitimate interests for security, audit logs, abuse prevention, product diagnostics, and service improvement;
- consent where required for marketing cookies, optional communications, or non-essential analytics;
- legal obligation for tax, accounting, dispute, and compliance records.

These bases must be validated by local counsel before launch in each target jurisdiction.

## 5. Telegram Data

Telegram is a separate third-party platform. When users interact with a Market Signal AI bot, channel, group, or WebApp, Telegram may process data under Telegram's own terms and privacy policy.

Market Signal AI may receive Telegram data when users:

- send messages or commands to a bot;
- open the Telegram WebApp;
- press buttons in bot messages;
- join or request access to a channel or group;
- interact in a group where a bot has access;
- use Telegram payment or platform features if enabled.

Users should not send sensitive personal information, account credentials, private keys, trading account passwords, or payment card details through Telegram messages.

## 6. Payments

Payments should be processed by a third-party payment provider such as Stripe, Telegram Payments, or another approved provider. Market Signal AI should not store full card numbers or CVV codes.

We may store payment status, plan, invoice references, customer IDs, subscription IDs, billing country, tax status, and dispute metadata needed to manage the subscription and comply with accounting obligations.

## 7. Sharing Data

We may share data with:

- hosting and infrastructure providers;
- database and logging providers;
- payment processors;
- Telegram and Telegram-related integrations;
- market data and news providers where needed;
- analytics and error monitoring providers;
- email and support tools;
- professional advisers;
- regulators, courts, or authorities where required by law.

We do not sell personal data as part of the current product model.

## 8. International Transfers

Data may be processed in countries other than the user's country. Cross-border transfer mechanisms must be reviewed before launch based on the operating entity, hosting region, provider list, and target user countries.

## 9. Retention

Recommended baseline retention:

- account data: while the account is active, then deleted or anonymized after a defined closure period;
- subscription and invoice records: retained as required by tax and accounting law;
- API usage logs: retained for security, billing, and abuse prevention for a limited period;
- audit logs: retained long enough for security and compliance investigation;
- Telegram interaction logs: retained only as needed for delivery, support, security, and product operation;
- analysis history: retained while the user account is active unless the user deletes it or retention settings provide otherwise;
- deleted account backups: removed according to the backup retention schedule.

Exact retention periods must be approved before launch.

## 10. User Rights

Depending on jurisdiction, users may have rights to:

- access their data;
- correct inaccurate data;
- delete data;
- restrict processing;
- object to processing;
- export data;
- withdraw consent;
- complain to a data protection authority.

The product must provide a support channel for privacy requests before launch.

## 11. Security

Market Signal AI should use reasonable technical and organizational safeguards, including access controls, secret management, encrypted transport, rate limits, audit logs, production/staging separation, and API key hashing.

No system is perfectly secure. Users are responsible for protecting their own account, Telegram account, devices, and API keys.

## 12. Cookies And Analytics

If the website uses cookies, analytics, advertising pixels, or similar tracking technologies, a Cookie Policy and cookie consent flow may be required. Non-essential cookies should not be enabled until the required consent model is implemented for target jurisdictions.

## 13. Children's Privacy

Market Signal AI is not intended for children. The minimum user age must be defined before launch based on the operating jurisdiction and target markets.

## 14. Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy. Material changes should be communicated to users where required by law or product policy.

## 15. Contact

Privacy contact details must be added before launch.

Local legal review required before launch:

- GDPR/UK GDPR applicability;
- CCPA/CPRA and other US state privacy law applicability;
- Israeli Privacy Protection Law if targeting Israeli users;
- Telegram bot privacy disclosure requirements;
- data processing agreements with vendors;
- cookie consent requirements;
- retention schedule and deletion workflow.
