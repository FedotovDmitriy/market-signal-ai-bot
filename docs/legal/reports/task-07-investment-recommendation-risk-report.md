## Delivered

- Identified high-risk features, risky language, safer positioning, output rules, required controls, and manager escalation triggers.

## Conclusion

The product can be positioned as informational SaaS analytics, but it must avoid directive, personalized, or profit-promising language.

## Must-Have Before Launch

- Ban "buy/sell/hold recommendation" language unless reviewed and approved by qualified local counsel.
- Keep all outputs framed as informational analysis.
- Do not use personal financial circumstances to produce recommendations.
- Require legal review before API redistribution or performance marketing claims.

## Escalation

Escalate to manager if product copy, Telegram content, or reports start instructing users to buy/sell/hold, promising returns, or using personal financial data to recommend trades.
